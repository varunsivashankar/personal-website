Varun Sivashankar

Princeton University
 

I am a PhD student in Applied and Computational Mathematics at Princeton University. I am advised by Matija Bucic and Noga Alon. I was a predoctoral research fellow at Microsoft Research in the Algorithms and Theory Group where my advisor was Ravishankar Krishnaswamy. I completed my undergraduate degree in mathematics from UCLA where my advisor was Amit Sahai.

My research interests include combinatorics, algorithms and theoretical computer science.

Here is my CV. My email is varunsiva@princeton.edu.
